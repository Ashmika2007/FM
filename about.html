Welcome to Fresh Mart

We are dedicated to providing the best services to our customers. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is unmatched. Here’s a look at our three branches and their locations.


Branch 1


Nestled in the scenic town of Nuwara Eliya, our supermarket offers a wide range of fresh produce, local specialties, and everyday essentials, providing a convenient shopping experience amidst the beautiful hill country



Branch 2



Situated in the historic city of Kandy, our supermarket provides a wide variety of fresh produce, local delicacies, and everyday necessities, catering to the needs of both residents and tourists in a convenient and welcoming environment.



Branch 3



Located in the bustling city of Colombo, our supermarket offers a diverse selection of international and local products, fresh produce, and household essentials, ensuring a convenient and comprehensive shopping experience for all customers.
